About Us

At Daebak Chikeng, we're passionate about serving up the crispiest, juiciest fried chicken in Terengganu. Our story began in 2016 when Mr Habib and Mrs Hajar set out to create a place where everyone could enjoy delicious, made-from-scratch fried chicken in a welcoming, casual, family-friendly atmosphere.
The secret to our incredible fried chicken lies in our unique marinade, breading, cooking method and sauces. We use only the freshest, high-quality ingredients to create a flavor that will have you coming back for more.
While our fried chicken is the star of the show, we also offer a variety of delicious sides and refreshing drinks to complete your meal.

Come join us and experience the Daebak Chikeng difference!
